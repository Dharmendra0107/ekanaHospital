FAQs

Common questions, answered.

Do you accept cashless insurance?

Yes, Ekana Medicare offers cashless facilities with most major insurance providers and TPAs. Our help desk can confirm your specific plan's coverage.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our appointment form, call our reception, or simply walk in to our OPD during working hours.

Is emergency care available 24/7?

Yes, our emergency department and ambulance services operate around the clock, every day of the year.

Do you offer health checkup packages?

Yes, we offer a range of preventive health packages tailored by age and risk profile. Ask our front desk for current options.
