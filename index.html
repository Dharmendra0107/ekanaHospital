About Ekana Medicare

An integrated hospital built
around the patient.

Located on Amar Shaheed Path in Gomti Nagar Extension, Ekana Medicare is a 100-bed multispeciality facility delivering advanced diagnostics, surgical care and round-the-clock emergency support to families across Lucknow — backed by senior consultants across 15+ specialities.

  • 24/7 ICU & NICU care
  • Full modular operation theatres
  • In-house pathology & radiology
  • On-call ambulance service
  • Dialysis & critical care units
  • Wheelchair-accessible campus

“स्वस्थ जीवन की ओर बढ़ाया हर कदम” — every step toward a healthier life.
