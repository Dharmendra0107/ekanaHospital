About Us

Where Healing Meets Innovation

Ekana Hospital Multispeciality Hospital & Research Center is an integrated and synergistic medical facility located at the heart of Gomti Nagar, Lucknow. With 100 beds and a team of senior consultants across 15+ specialties, we provide accessible, compassionate, and cutting-edge medical care to patients from across Uttar Pradesh and beyond.

NICU & ICU
