About Ekana Medicare

Care that combines clinical depth with genuine warmth

Since opening our doors in Gomti Nagar Extension, Ekana Medicare Multispeciality Hospital & Research Center has grown into one of Lucknow's most trusted healthcare destinations — built on three principles: experienced specialists, modern infrastructure, and a patient comes first philosophy.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, evidence-based multispeciality care with compassion, transparency, and respect for every patient who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be Uttar Pradesh's most trusted research-driven hospital, setting the benchmark for clinical excellence and patient experience.
