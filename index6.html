FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment at Ekana Medicare?
You can book an appointment by calling our helpline at +91 98765 43210, using the online appointment form on this website, or visiting our reception desk directly. We also offer same-day appointments for urgent cases.
What insurance companies are you empanelled with?
We are empanelled with all major insurance providers including Star Health, New India, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, ICICI Lombard, and many others. We also accept government schemes like CGHS, ECHS, and Ayushman Bharat.
What are your visiting hours for admitted patients?
Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM daily. ICU patients have restricted visits; please contact our nursing station for special arrangements.
Do you offer telemedicine or online consultation services?
Yes, we offer teleconsultation services for follow-up appointments and non-emergency consultations. You can book a video consultation through our portal or by calling our helpline. Available Monday–Saturday, 9 AM–6 PM.
Is parking available at the hospital?
Yes, we have a spacious multi-level parking facility with dedicated spaces for disabled patients and emergency vehicles. Parking is available for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers with reasonable charges.
